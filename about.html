Meet The Co-Hosts

20Something & Living is a podcast featuring co-hosts, Therese, 27, and Krista, 26. Therese is frank and sarcastic while Krista is passionate and persistent, an odd mix. But for these two -- it works! Their chemistry makes for an entertaining bunch with lots of laughs! The podcast speaks to women like them, 20Something and finding their way. They premiere a new episode every other Tuesday on iTunes & Soundcloud.

Krista

Krista was born and raised in St. Louis. She is a quirky, passionate, young woman who is growing closer to God everyday. She is an alumna of the University of Missouri (Mizzou). Currently, she resides in Cleveland working in television and media. She is a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc, enjoys volunteering at her church, and always looking for a new restaurant to try. The things she holds closest are advocating for your goals, uplifting the black community, practicing self-care, adoring your friends/family and keeping God first.

Therese

Therese was born and raised in St. Louis. She is a down-to-earth, sarcastic woman who is always up for a good laugh. She is a recent graduate of the University of Central Missouri, School of Business. Currently, she works in Management in Cleveland. She is the living definition of a cultural Foodie, and a lover of all things - Yelp. When she isn’t in the kitchen cooking or getting updated on pop culture, you can find her on youtube researching conspiracy theories and random facts. As of right now, she says she is taking life one day at a time, all the while trying to get it together.
